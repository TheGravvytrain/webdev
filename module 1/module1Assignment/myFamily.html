Immediate Family...

My immediate family consists of just my mom and my brother, which are the two people I am closest to in my entire family. My mom is a very funny, outgoing, and very caring individual who has always provided me with countless laughs and motivation throughout all my hard years in school and any other activity I become interested in. She is my rock and the person I can always turn to for advice and I love her more than she probably realized (she gets on to me a lot!).
Of course, I can't talk about just my mom when my brother is just as close to me. He may get on my nerves sometimes, well he gets on my nerves a lot really, but despite that he has become my absolute best friend. We pretty much do everything together such as fishing or gaming and even go to Walmart or the grocery store together for our mom! He's such a character and always makes me and everyone laugh and he has a very big heart, I love him to death and I know he will always be right there by my side!

My immediate family is my ride or die!

Other, Close Family...

The most important individuals of my the rest of my family are definetely my grandparents. Despite their old age, they still manage to come across with youthful exhuberance the majority of the time. My grandfather has easily been one of the most admired people in my life in the ways that he is always trying to help others in any way he can and through his amazing work ethic, which I still try to replicate myself. My grandmother, unlike my grandfather who is usually pretty stern, is super laid-back and always in a funny or joking mood to which she always keeps me laughing with her jokes or her pretty unusual stories that she likes to tell me of her youth. I love both of them more than anything as they have been one of the biggest helps in my life.
Lastly, but not least, would be my uncle. My uncle has been one of my bestfriends, despite being my uncle, since the day I was born (we were born one day apart). He has probably been the closest thing I have had to a father figure in my life and he has never taught me wrong and I would trust him with my life. He is super funny and very energetic and we are always getting into some stuff from time to time. We share a lot of the same hobbies and I think that helps us make a much stronger connection. I love him with all my heart just as I know he does with me.

Just because they are my "other" family doesn't mean I love them any less!
