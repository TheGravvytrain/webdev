Outdoor Hobbies...

  • Fishing: I typically do a lot of fishing, especially come Spring time and most of the time all throughout summer as well. Most of the time I do freshwater fishing in ponds and lakes, but every now and then I like to go salt water fishing on the coast with my brother or a couple of my buddies. We always have a good time whether we catch any fish or not!

  • Driving: Believe it or not, I actually really enjoy driving/riding around town and taking in the local sights. Typically my girlfriend and I will just hop in the car, mostly at night when theres no traffic, and just see what we can see or do. Our favorite places to ride around are Hattiesburg, Ocean Springs, and even further away places like Florida or Alabama beaches such as Pensacola or Orange Beach. There are always new and interesting places and activities to do if you just look a little harder than usual!

  • Skating or Hiking: A few summers ago I decided to pick up skateboarding as a small hobby to keep my active, and I never looked back. Since then, I have made an effort to skate around twice a week during the Summer months, sometimes even more than that. I also really enjoy hiking for a lot of the reasons that I enjoy riding around town, there are just so many sights to see and it can be very peaceful and thereputic. There aren't many hiking areas in the surrounding cities so I typically hike through some of the coastal trails in Ocean Springs.

It is because of this facination that I have also developed a love for tinkering with these systems and fixing broken and old systems and game cartridges.
